About Me

Following a long career in Fashion, I decided to take a different direction in my life, culminating in moving back to Ireland with my partner and little son Fionn. I have always had an interest in cake baking but never found the time. Now having settled in Ireland in 2017 I have been developing this idea and to date have been working via word of mouth. I totally love the art of making and creating something special for somebody on their special occasions.

Thanks you for taking the time to browse through my website.
